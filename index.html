RMate

UCR School App

Incoming freshmen, transfer students, and school visitors all have one thing in common, they all are lost when they first arrive.

RMate is a UCR school app. Students are able to navigate to their classrooms and visitors can navigate to buildings. Both can navigate to any school events. School faculty are able to engage with students and vise versa.

RMate is the successor of my previous project UCRMap, now created using React Native and Javascript in order to support Android and iOS mobile platforms.
